TECHNICAL SUPPORT POINTS
========================

Common Questions Customers Will Ask:

Q: "What GPU do I need?"
A: Any NVIDIA RTX 20-series or newer. We ship with support for 
   CUDA 7.0+ (RTX 2010 through RTX 4090).

Q: "Does it work on AMD/Intel GPUs?"
A: Currently NVIDIA-only. AMD and Intel support planned for v2.0.

Q: "Can I run it without a GPU?"
A: The solver requires NVIDIA GPU (no CPU fallback). This ensures 
   maximum performance for our customers.

Q: "What's the problem size limit?"
A: Up to 50,000 variables (memory-limited). Larger problems may 
   require data center hardware (additional SKU).

Q: "Can I modify the algorithm?"
A: The core solver is proprietary (obfuscated binary). But you can 
   customize hyperparameters (thermal_melt, patience, etc.).

Q: "Will there be updates?"
A: Yes. License includes one year of updates. Extended support 
   available at additional cost.

Q: "What about data privacy?"
A: Completely offline. No data leaves the customer's machine. 
   REST API is local-only (localhost:8765).
